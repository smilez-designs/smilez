Campaign / Marketing

Sabah Brand Work

A collection of brand, marketing, and editorial design work created for Sabah, including email campaigns, in-store collateral, event graphics, and visual storytelling.

Role

Graphic Design / Marketing Design / Brand Support

Scope

Email design, print collateral, event graphics, visual assets

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Klaviyo

Overview

This work focused on supporting Sabah’s brand across multiple touchpoints, from digital campaigns to physical retail materials. The goal was to create assets that felt visually consistent with the brand while still being flexible across different formats and events.

Approach

The design direction balanced clean layouts with strong typography and brand-led storytelling. Each piece needed to feel warm, refined, and visually engaging while still being practical for marketing use.
